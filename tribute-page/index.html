The People's Youtuber

Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg, better known as PewDiePie, is a renowned Swedish youtuber known for his comedic content creation with Let's Play videos. Besides his youtube content creation, PewDiePie also published a couple of graphic novels. PewDiePie currently has 110 million subscribers to his Youtube Channel with each of his vidoes reaching a million views every time.

Novels

In 2015 and 2019 PewDiePie published two different novels titled This Book Loves You. These books were written as a parody of self help books and contains comedic graphics. He has sold over 200,000 copies of his novel.
